Turbo Strong Coffee

Description

If you need some nicer coffee, here you go

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of coffee, 200 ml glass at least
  • 1/2 cup of milk, from same container that the coffee was in
  • 1 teaspoon of cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of xylitol (so it is drinkable)

Steps

Coffee itself

  1. Get some robusta mix - preferably 50% of over in the mix.
  2. Put that into espresso maker - mix cinnamon in between.
  3. Get the coffee.

The rest of the plan

  1. You've got coffee now.
    First put cocoa inside and stir hard.
  2. Now, put sugar inside, stir hard again
  3. Get the milk and pour it into mixture
