Apple Pie Moonshine

Description

Best moonshine you ever drank!

You will never forget the experience... (until you will)

Ingredients

  • 4 liters apple juice
  • 4 liters apple cider
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 4 cinnamon sticks, or more, as you want
  • 1 pinch applie pie spice
  • 1 liter bottle of 95% ethanol
  • 1 liter of vodka

Steps

  1. Combine all but ethanol thingies into large pot, almost boil it inside.
    Cover pot with lid, reduce heat and simmer for about an hour.
    Remove from heat and cool completely.
  2. Stir 95% ethanol with vodka, pour into apple syrup.
    Remove cinnamon sticks.
  3. Pour apple pie moonshine into clean glass jars or bottles. Store in refrigerator.
